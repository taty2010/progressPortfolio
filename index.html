About Me

I currently reside in Texas with my husband and fur-baby. I recieved my BFA from the Univeristy of Texas at Arlington but have always had an dream to become a developer. I first learned about coding almost 14 years ago and ever since then I've wanted to learn how to build a website from the ground up, but I initially didn't know where to start.

I am currenly taking an online course with Skillcrush and am using other resources that are giving me the knowledge that I need, which wll help me to one day become a full-time Front End Developer. I hope to use this page as a portfolio to not only see the progress for myself but to show people that you can do anything once you put your mind to it.
